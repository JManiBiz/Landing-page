Traditional piano feel

We at Songbird Entertainment guarantee that each keyboard is hand-crafted with care to enhance your stage performance. The Metronome SB keyboard comes complete with 88 ivory keys that are fully weighted to replicate a traditional grand piano feel.

High-Quality Sound and Easy Sound Control

Thanks to its sophisticated sound engine and precise digital sampling, the Metronome SB keyboard has the highest quality of sound. Its velocity sensitivity can sense how hard you strike the keys and matches your striking intensity allowing for a variation in volume. The intuitive interface is versatile giving you quick and easy splits, layers and sound-shaping directly from the front panel.

Design

From being bumped, jostled and scratched to being stuffed in overhead bins, our keyboards are made to withstand the wear and tear from constant use. See-through drawbar stops with LED lights enhance visibility making it easy to see the differences between simulated upper and lower configuration and play in split mode. There multiple output ports including a built-in USB stereo audio and MIDI interface for Mac, PC and IOS devices.
